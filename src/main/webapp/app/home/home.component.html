Welcome to Inidus Platform Sandbox

The Inidus Platform Sandbox provides an open sandbox environment for anyone who wants to discover, demonstrate, test or build health and care solutions by simulating a wide range of open systems and services you are increasingly likely to find in UK health and care.


The Inidus Platform Sandbox is for non-operational use only and does not store any personal data. Access to the data and services available via the Inidus Platform is free to all registered users subject to agreement with a simple set of terms and conditions. The Inidus Platform includes items that may also be freely available for use in an operational environment, while the operational use other items may require that you enter into a commercial agreement with the owner.
